Welcome Message from Principal

“Education is a natural process carried out by the child and is not acquired by listening to words but by experiences in the environment.” If a child is interested, he will learn, and his learning will be deep and lasting.

Our classrooms are filled with beautiful materials, enticing choices, experienced and dedicated teachers and other interested learners. This combination creates a powerful experience unlike any other learning environment and classroom.

The mission of Oak Grove Montessori School is to provide a nurturing, high quality Montessori educational program in which children can realize their potential. This mission is shared by parents, teachers and staff alike. It is our hope to assist children in becoming responsible, resourceful and respectful.

Oak Grove is more than a school. It is a strong community of families with common goals and values.

As Head of School my role of leadership and my goals for the school are many. The most important however, is to provide a safe, nurturing learning environment filled with opportunities for each student to meet their unique potential, be challenged and learn according to their own style. Every child has a gift and it is our responsibility to find the ways to make those gifts emerge. We want each child to understand their strengths and to acknowledge their weaknesses and to know what it takes to succeed each day and to be assured that they each have their gifts to share with the world.

We have seen the fruits of our work in the amazing results of our graduates, as they step into the next chapter of their lives as self-assured, successful students and leaders. Thank you for visiting our website. I invite you to call and set up a time to visit and tour our school and meet our staff.
